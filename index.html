Privacy Policy — Tend

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Tend is a calm routine tracker for iPhone. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information. Short version: Tend does not collect, store, or share your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Information we collect

None. Tend has no account system, no login, and includes no analytics or third-party tracking software. The app makes no network connections of its own. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Information that stays on your device

Everything you do in Tend is stored locally on your device and is never sent to us or anyone else:

  • Your routines and check-ins — the routines you create, your daily check-ins and skips, sub-steps, and your consistency history are stored on your device using Apple's on-device database.
  • Reminders — reminder times and the days you choose are stored on your device and scheduled as local notifications by iOS. They are not sent to a server.
  • Widget data — to show today's progress in the Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets, Tend shares a small snapshot of your routines with its own widget through a private app group container on your device. This data never leaves your device.

Purchases

Tend offers a one-time in-app purchase, Tend Plus, that unlocks additional features. Payment is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store; Tend never sees or stores your payment details. Whether you own the unlock is determined on your device through Apple's StoreKit, and that information is not transmitted to us.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Tend uses them only to send the gentle reminders you set up, and a single "focus complete" nudge after a focus session. Notification content is generated on your device. You can turn notifications off at any time in the iOS Settings app.

Health-related information

Tend is designed for adults with ADHD, so the routines you track may reflect personal or health-related habits. Tend does not use Apple HealthKit and does not read or write any data to the Health app. Anything you enter stays on your device, under your control, and is never transmitted to us.

Deleting your data

You can delete any routine inside the app at any time. Deleting the app from your device removes all of its stored data, including your routines, check-ins, and history.

Children

Tend is a general-purpose routine tracker and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
