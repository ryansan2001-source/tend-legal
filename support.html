Common questions

Where is my data stored?

Entirely on your device. Tend has no account and makes no network connections of its own — your routines, check-ins, and history never leave your iPhone. See the privacy policy for details.

How do I turn reminders on or off?

Open Settings inside Tend to enable notifications, and edit any routine to set reminder times and days. You can also manage Tend's notification permission anytime in the iOS Settings app under Notifications → Tend.

The widget isn't updating

Open Tend once so it can refresh today's snapshot, then remove and re-add the widget if needed. The widget rolls over to a new day automatically after midnight.

How do I delete my data?

Delete individual routines inside the app, or remove the app from your device to erase everything Tend has stored.
